Colored glasses and cancan dance


Oh, the world is not a beautiful place.
It's not just bright forever.
I want to keep the abnormal scenery tied up and wear beautiful colored glasses.
If you do the dance perfectly, I won't come back anymore.
Please let me take your bruise with me.
I gave you my sunglasses, so is the price enough?
Even if it wasn't a parallel relationship, I'd have to say the last word.
Live by adding color to your gaze.
Don't leave your body to the painful dance anymore.
